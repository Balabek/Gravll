Our Story

Unbreaking The Industry

Gravll was born out of a place of necessity. My business partners and I had become incredibly frustrated with the lack of customer service and transparency within the auto repair and maintenance industry. Too many times I’ve heard stories where customers felt like numbers instead of people. Auto repair shops have tried to upsell customers with services they don’t need in an effort to increase shop revenues and profit margins. Taking your vehicle to a dealership is almost worse than a repair shop because customers are ultimately paying for all the overhead. Substantially all the dealerships’ marketing, advertising, rent, etc. are baked into the blended hourly rates charged for services.

The wait times have become unbearable and scheduling appointments beforehand doesn’t seem to increase the service delivery speed. The majority of the industry appears to have forgotten they’re in the customer service business. The entire business model is centered around the business, not the customer. We at Gravll firmly believe there are plenty of talented professional working within the auto repair and maintenance industry, they’ve just been forced to operate within a broken customer service model. Gravll aims to change all of this, we’re here to “unbreak” the industry.

Our Philosophy

Committed to Quality

Our company was built on the philosophy that you should treat people the way you want to be treated. To that end, we’re committed to doing what we say we’re going to do, when we say we’re going to do it. The greatest resource an individual has is their time. Everything we do from a service delivery perspective is an attempt to give people back their time. Our commitment to providing quality repair, maintenance, and customer service is unwavering. When people ask what sets Gravll apart from other service providers, the answer is quality.

The values and respect we have for our customers extends to all our employees, vendors, and independent contractors. We recognize these individuals have options when choosing where to work and with whom, we’re committed to providing them with unique, challenging, and rewarding career opportunities. We’re not interested in one-off transactions with vendors or temporary employees, we firmly believe in the value of building lasting relationships. For more information on current openings, head over to our Careers page.
